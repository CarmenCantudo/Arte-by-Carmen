Faqs

To place an order, simply browse our website and select the products that interest you. Once you have found a design that you like, or prefer a completely custom design, you can write to us by email or WhatsApp to tell us what products you want. We will explain the entire process and the next steps to design your perfect wedding stationery.

The production and shipping time varies depending on the product you have ordered and depending on the revisions or changes we make. Our custom products, such as wedding invitations and stamps, require additional production time to ensure every detail is printed and cut to the highest quality. The approximate production times once the digital proofs are approved are as follows:
  • Creation: 4-5 business days to create
  • Printing: 4-5 business days after creation
  • Subsequent production of finishes/decorations: variable according to each order
Once we have produced your order, we will send it to you by the courier company within approximately 48-72 hours.

Once you have paid for your order and approved the digital proof of your design, we cannot accept changes. However, if you need to make any changes before approving the digital proof, we will be in contact beforehand and will do our best to help you.

Yes, we offer digital proofs of our designs before you place your order. Sample samples and photos can help you evaluate the quality of our products and decide which design is best for you. We'll send you a digital proof of your finished design before we proceed to printing and production so you can review it and make sure everything is correct.

After the printing and production process, we carry out a thorough review to evaluate the quality of our products and check that everything is perfect to be shipped. However, if you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will be happy to help you and find a suitable solution.
